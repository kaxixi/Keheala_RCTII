# Study team contact-queue protocol (Keheala arm)

This file summarizes the protocol used by the Keheala study team to contact
participants who failed to self-verify medication adherence via the platform's
automated prompts. The full operational document is pending release from
Keheala, Inc.

## Queue entry criterion

Participants in the Keheala arm were placed on a queue for contact by a
member of the study team if, within one hour of sending the third verification
request, they still had not verified.

## Contact modality

Study team members contacted queued participants by **SMS or phone call**.
The specific modality, cadence, and escalation rules — along with guidance on
tone, logging, and when to involve the participant's clinic — are part of the
study team's training manual and are pending release from Keheala.

## Participant-initiated contact

Participants could also initiate an asynchronous conversation with the study
team via a chat feature to, e.g., request a phone call, ask questions about
treatment, raise concerns, or obtain assistance communicating with clinic
staff.
